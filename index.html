About Us

At NEO Tracker, we are dedicated to the exploration and understanding of Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) – asteroids and comets that come close to Earth’s orbit. Our mission is to provide an engaging platform for space enthusiasts, researchers, and educators to visualize, track, and learn about these celestial bodies in real-time.

We strive to promote awareness about these objects, their potential impact on Earth, and the fascinating science behind their behavior. Join us on a journey to explore the vastness of our solar system, and understand how NEOs affect our planet.
